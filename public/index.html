⚡ How It Works
🎱 5×5 Bingo
1
Join a Round — Pay 50 coins. Each player gets a unique 5×5 card generated from a provably fair seed.
2
Numbers Called — Every 4 seconds a number 1–75 is called. The full board shows which are called.
3
Mark Your Card — Tap a number on your card when it's called. FREE centre is auto-marked.
4
Shout BINGO! — Complete a Row, Column, Diagonal, or Full House, then tap BINGO! to win the pot.
5
Verify Fairness — After the game the server reveals the seed. Anyone can verify the card and call order were not manipulated.
